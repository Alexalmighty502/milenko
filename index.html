What is Milenko?

Milenko is an "anti-bot bot" (yes, really...) that helps to remove malicious bots from Team Fortress 2 casual matches by detecting, reporting, votekicking, and killing them in the interim.
In other words, Milenko bots are "Friendly Botkillers".
Their name stems from the song "The Great Milenko" by Insane Clown Posse.
There have been malicious bots impersonating Milenko under fake names like "mlienko". These bots are not affiliated with the Milenko project.

Will the botkillers leave once all the malicious bots are kicked from the match?

Yes, they will. They stay in every new match for a brief period (about 30 seconds after setup time ends) to detect malicious bots, but they will leave automatically. You do not need to kick them!

How can I tell the difference between Milenko and malicious bots?

Milenko has some unique features and behaviors that can help to distinguish them from malicious bots:

  • Milenko bots will join as the spy class.
  • When a bot is detected, they will switch to the sniper class and look straight up.
  • Milenko bots will only kill malicious bots.
  • When a votekick is called, Milenko bots will automatically vote Yes on malicious bots and No on regular players.

Milenko bots that have been upgraded to premium will:

  • Announce a list of current malicious bots in the chat periodically
  • Automatically call votekicks against malicious bots on the same team
  • When a malicious bot joins or is first detected, Milenko bots will say "Sentry ahead!" as a warning.
