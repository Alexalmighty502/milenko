What is Milenko?

"Milenko's Bot Detector" is an "anti-bot bot" (yes, really...) that helps to remove malicious bots from Team Fortress 2 casual matches by detecting, reporting, votekicking, and killing them in the interim.
In other words, Milenko bots are "Friendly Botkillers".
Their name stems from the song "The Great Milenko" by Insane Clown Posse.

Will the botkillers leave once all the malicious bots are kicked from the match?

Yes, they will. They stay in every new match for a brief period (about 10 seconds after setup time ends) to detect malicious bots, but they will leave automatically. You do not need to kick them!

How can I tell the difference between Milenko and malicious bots?

Milenko has some unique features and behaviors that can help to distinguish them from malicious bots:

  • Milenko bots will join as the spy class and "spycrab" in the spawn room.
  • When a malicious bot is detected, they will switch to the sniper class and target them.
  • Milenko bots will only kill malicious bots.
  • When a votekick is called, Milenko bots will automatically vote Yes on malicious bots and No on regular players.
  • Most Milenko bots will not wear any cosmetic items.

Milenko bots that have been upgraded to premium will:

  • Announce a list of current malicious bots in the chat periodically
  • Automatically call votekicks against malicious bots on the same team
  • When votekicks are called, they will announce in chat whether that player is a bot
  • When a malicious bot is detected, Milenko bots will say "Sentry ahead!" periodically as a warning
  • Perform the Disco Fever taunt when they join
  • Wear the Mann Co. hat
