What is Milenko?

"Milenko's Bot Detector" is an "anti-bot bot" (yes, really...) that helps to remove malicious bots from Team Fortress 2 casual matches by detecting, reporting, votekicking, and killing them in the interim.
In other words, Milenko bots are "Friendly Botkillers".
Their name stems from the song "The Great Milenko" by Insane Clown Posse.

What does Milenko do?

  • Milenko bots join casual matches in Team Fortress 2 and automatically detect malicious bots.
  • Milenko bots will join as the spy class and "spycrab" in the spawn room.
  • When a malicious bot is detected in a match, Milenko switches to the sniper class and targets these bots.
  • Milenko bots will only kill malicious bots.
  • This helps to prevent malicious bots from ruining the fun of innocent players and makes it more obvious when malicious bots are present.

  • Premium Milenko bots will announce a list of the malicious bots on each team in the chat periodically.
  • This makes it much easier for teams to coordinate votekicks on malicious bots.

  • When possible (not on 2 minute votekick cooldown), premium milenko bots will automatically call votekicks against malicious bots on the same team.

  • They will also inform players in chat whether or not the player being kicked is a known bot.
  • This helps to prevent innocent players from being kicked by malicious bots or other players.
  • When a votekick is called, Milenko bots will automatically vote Yes on malicious bots and No on regular players.

Will Milenko bots leave once all the malicious bots are kicked from the match?

Yes, they will. They stay in every new match for a brief period (about 15 seconds after setup time ends) to detect malicious bots, but they will leave automatically. You do not need to kick them!

How can I tell the difference between Milenko and malicious bots?

Milenko bots have some features and behaviors that can help to distinguish them from malicious bots:

  • About half of Milenko bots will not wear any cosmetic items due to not being premium.
  • When no malicious bots are detected, Milenko bots will "explode" before leaving.
  • After killing the last malicious bot on the other team, Milenko bots will taunt with the "Director's Vision".

Milenko bots that have been upgraded to premium will:

  • Perform the Disco Fever taunt when they join
  • Always wear the Mann Co. hat
  • When a malicious bot is detected, Milenko bots will say "Sentry ahead!" every 15 seconds as a warning
